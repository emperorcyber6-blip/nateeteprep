About Us

A Foundation for Success

Nateete Preparatory School provides an engaging, supportive environment where children discover their potential.

Located in Lubaga Division, Kampala, we have built a reputation for academic rigor and holistic development. Our dedicated staff ensures every child receives the attention required to excel both inside and outside the classroom.

  • Safe Environment: Secure campus ensuring peace of mind.
  • Experienced Teachers: Qualified educators passionate about learning.
  • Holistic Approach: Balancing academics with co-curriculars.

Our Strengths

Why Parents Choose Us

We go beyond standard curriculums to provide an enriching educational experience tailored to the modern world.

Academic Excellence

A proven track record of outstanding performance in primary leaving examinations, preparing students for top secondary schools in the nation.

Moral Foundations

We instill core values of integrity, respect, and discipline, ensuring our students grow into responsible citizens.

Dedicated Staff

Our teachers are not just educators; they are mentors fully committed to nurturing the unique talents of each individual learner.

Learning Journey

Comprehensive Curriculum

We offer a structured progression from Nursery through Primary Seven, aligned with the National Curriculum (UNEB).

Our teaching methodology emphasizes interactive learning, ensuring students grasp concepts practically rather than through rote memorization alone. Subjects span mathematics, sciences, languages, and the arts.
