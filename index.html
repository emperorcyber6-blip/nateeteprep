About Our School

A Foundation for Lifelong Learning

Nateete Preparatory School provides an exceptional educational environment where every child is encouraged to reach their full potential.

Located in the Lubaga Division of Kampala, our institution is dedicated to nurturing young minds through a balanced curriculum, dedicated teachers, and a supportive community. We pride ourselves on creating a safe space where students thrive academically, socially, and emotionally.

Our Curriculum

Comprehensive Academics

From the early years in Nursery to our rigorous Primary Leaving Examination (PLE) preparation, our curriculum is designed to challenge and inspire.

  • Nursery Section: Play-based learning and early literacy.
  • Lower Primary: Building foundational math and language skills.
  • Upper Primary: Critical thinking, science, and leadership.
  • PLE Prep: Intensive, targeted guidance for national exams.
