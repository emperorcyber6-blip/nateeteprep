How to Apply

Admission Process

  1. 1

    Submit Application

    Download our application form or pick one up from our school office. Fill it out completely and attach the required documents.

  2. 2

    Interview & Assessment

    Once your application is reviewed, the student will be invited for a brief academic assessment and a parent interview.

  3. 3

    Admission Decision

    You will be notified of the admission decision within one week. Successful applicants will receive an acceptance letter.
