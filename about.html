About Our School

A tradition of excellence, nurturing young minds in Nateete since day one.

Our History

A Legacy of Excellence

Nateete Preparatory School was established with a singular focus: to provide accessible, high-quality primary education to the children of the Lubaga Division and beyond. Over the years, we have grown from a modest neighborhood institution into a recognized center of foundational learning in Kampala.

Our commitment to rigorous academic standards is reflected in our consistent performance in national examinations, standing as a testament to the hard work of our dedicated staff and students.

Purpose & Direction

Guiding Principles

Our Mission

To provide a holistic education that empowers learners to excel academically, morally, and socially, preparing them to be responsible global citizens.

Our Vision

To be the premier preparatory school in Uganda, recognized for nurturing well-rounded individuals equipped for continuous lifelong learning.

Core Values

Excellence, Integrity, Discipline, Respect, and Teamwork. These pillars guide every interaction and decision within our school community.

Campus Life

Our Facilities

We believe that a conducive learning environment is crucial for student success. Our campus is designed to support both academic rigor and physical development.

  • Spacious Classrooms: Well-ventilated spaces conducive to focused learning.
  • Library Resources: Access to a wide range of reading materials to foster literacy.
  • Play Areas: Safe and designated spaces for sports and recreation.
  • Health & Safety: Sick bay facilities and strict campus security protocols.
