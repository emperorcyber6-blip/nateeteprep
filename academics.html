Our Academics

A comprehensive curriculum designed to discover and unearth potential.

Learning Stages

Educational Journey

We provide a seamless progression of learning, tailored to the developmental needs of every child at every stage.

Nursery Section

Providing a strong foundation through play-based learning, early literacy, numeracy, and social interaction. We nurture curiosity and creativity in our youngest learners.

Lower Primary

P1 to P3 focuses on building core foundational competencies in Mathematics, English, Literacy, and practical skills in a supportive, engaging environment.

Upper Primary

P4 to P6 introduces a broader curriculum including Integrated Science and Social Studies, developing critical thinking, independent learning, and leadership skills.

PLE Preparation

P7 is dedicated to intensive preparation for the Primary Leaving Examinations (UNEB). We provide specialized coaching, regular assessments, and mentorship.

Beyond Books

Co-curricular Activities

Education at Nateete goes beyond the classroom. We offer a vibrant co-curricular program ensuring students develop physically, socially, and creatively.

  • Sports & Athletics: Football, netball, and track events.
  • Performing Arts: Traditional dance, music, and drama.
  • Clubs & Societies: Debating club, Science club, and Scouts/Girl Guides.

Teaching Methodology

Our approach is learner-centered. We believe in:

  • Interactive Learning: Encouraging student participation and inquiry.
  • Continuous Assessment: Regular tracking of student progress to provide timely support.
  • Holistic Development: Focusing on cognitive, emotional, and physical growth.
